# Quants Report Project Manifesto

## Purpose
Quants Report exists to help traders make better decisions by combining deterministic market intelligence with explainable AI.

## Vision
Build an AI-first market intelligence platform that scales from novice traders to professionals through Progressive Disclosure.

## Core Principles
- Database-centric architecture.
- AI assists but never fabricates market facts.
- Humans remain in control of order execution.
- Widgets are independent, movable and context-aware.
- Every decision should be explainable and auditable.

## Long-term Goal
Deliver a trusted market intelligence platform that is lightweight, secure, powerful, and enjoyable to use.
